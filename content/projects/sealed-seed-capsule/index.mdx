Hide metal seed phrase in a fully sealed plastic housing.

24 stamped stainles steel washers are push fit into the capsule over bump features designed to restrain the stack when complete. 

The lid is spun using a hand drill to spin-weld the parts together, sealing the stack in a polymer housing.
